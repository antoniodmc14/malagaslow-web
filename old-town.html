Historic Center

Navigate the charming, narrow streets of the Historic Quarter, where tour buses can’t reach, uncovering centuries of history at a pace that allows you to truly breathe it in. We will wander through the shady corridors of the Alcazaba, admire the 'La Manquita' Cathedral from its most secret angles, and lose ourselves in the vibrant Roman Theatre surroundings, ending with the hidden Moorish architecture that defines the true essence of Málaga.

90 min 110 €

Water included

Safety

Our tours are safe, secure, and suitable for children from 4 years old, ensuring a worry-free experience in the historic center.
